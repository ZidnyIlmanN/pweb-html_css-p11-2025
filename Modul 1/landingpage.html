About Me

I’m Tony Stark — genius, billionaire, philanthropist, and yes, the guy who built an armored suit in a cave… with a box of scraps.

I’ve spent my life pushing the limits of technology, not because I had to, but because no one else would. Innovation isn’t just what I do — it’s who I am.

Sure, I’ve made mistakes — a lot of them. But every failure only sharpened my resolve to create solutions that matter.

Whether it’s designing advanced suits, developing clean energy, or building an AI that’s a little too smart for its own good, I never stop moving forward.

Because at the end of the day, it’s not just about the armor. It’s about the man who wears it… and the legacy he leaves behind.
